Help me help you

If you're reporting a problem, including these makes the fix much faster:

  1. Your iPhone model and iOS version
  2. The app version (shown in Settings inside the app)
  3. What happened — and what you expected to happen
  4. Steps to reproduce it, if you can
  5. A screenshot, if it's something visual

Feature ideas

Requests genuinely shape the roadmap — several features in the app exist because someone emailed about them. If something would make your learning better, tell me.

Enjoying the app?

A review on the App Store is the single most helpful thing you can do for a small independent app. Thank you.
